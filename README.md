## Generic Workflow Management System 

Gen-Flow is the solution to the common challenge faced by small businesses in managing workflows effectively. With a focus on simplicity, Gen-Flow offers customizable workflow management, monitoring, and reporting capabilities tailored to the specific needs of any small-scale business.
